Synopsis

Cool Wallet S is a bluetooth hardware wallet for Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, XRP, Bitcoin Cash, and ERC20 Tokens. I had the chance to design for CoinGate, who is one of the first retailers of this cryptocurrency wallet in Japan. The purpose is to create a visually appealing product description page with a purchase option.

Iterative Design

CoinGate wanted to create an impactful visual when users land to the product page via ads, promotional flyers, or word of mouth. I worked closely with the CEO and we brainstormed together on different ideas. Since their target audience was in a fairly young age group, I decided to go for a more modern approach where a simple design that says less is more.

Event in Tokyo

I also helped design CoinGate's flyer for one of the largest Cryptocurrency Exhibition in Tokyo, in which they participated.
