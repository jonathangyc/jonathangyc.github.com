Why hello again. I'm Jonathan Chen, an UI & UX Designer. My focuses are web, mobile, and application design.

About me

Storyboarding

As both a consumer and a maker, I know the importance of being able to imagine who is using your product. User-stories allow myself (and others) to not lose sight of who the design is for.

Wireframing

From low-fi to hi-fi, I've done a lot of wireframes that outlines the basic structure of the user interface for both websites and applications.

Responsive Web Design

With different devices and screen sizes, I've come to design various types of websites responsively, thus, causing me to also design in the browser.

Prototyping

Having coding knowledge gives me the ability to prototype ideas, whether it's for interactivity or just getting a general feel of the design.

Communicating

I always try to talk with my users, clients, or anyone. This may seem obvious, but I believe without constant communication, things get lost-in-translation and misunderstandings happen.

Gaming

One of the ways in which I train myself to problem solve within a time limit, how to survive under pressure, and all around a good stress reliever.
