Synopsis

BuildingConnected is a construction communication platform with a refreshingly different approach. I had the wonderful chance of helping them out in their early beta stages of reskinning their homepage and web application. Here's to a prosperous future for the company.

Reskinning the Young Product

As the product was in a pretty early stage, my main task was to reskin the UI and incorporate more of their brand throughout their web application. By working closely with the founders of the company, we communicated about what BuildingConnected was looking for and what they wanted their customers to feel when using their product.
